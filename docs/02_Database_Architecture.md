## Brand Intelligence

Tracks real brands, positioning, product categories, public contact paths, social presence, source URLs, and data quality.

## Product Intelligence

Tracks real product-level records when product pages or reputable retailer pages verify product details.

## Market Intelligence

Tracks category trends, country distribution, price ranges, fabric usage, and opportunity signals.

## Product Decision Center

Converts research into product priorities, recommended actions, and sample planning.

## Sample Development

Uses selected product opportunities to guide sample categories, material choices, construction requirements, and difficulty estimates.

## B2B Website

Uses validated research and sample priorities to shape customer-facing content, product offers, and sales positioning.
