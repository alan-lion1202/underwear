# Project Principles

## Purpose

This document defines the highest-level rules for the LGBTQ+ underwear intelligence system. It is the first reference for product, data, UI, and engineering decisions.

## Mission

This project exists to continuously research global LGBTQ+ underwear brands, products, and market trends in order to identify the most valuable products to develop and support the long-term growth of our B2B brand.

## Product Vision

The database exists to support product decisions.

It is not built for collecting data for its own sake.

Every page, field, score, and feature must help improve product decisions.

## Four Design Principles

Before adding any feature, ask:

1. Does it help us understand the market better?
2. Does it help us decide which products are worth developing?
3. Does it help us serve future B2B customers better?
4. Does it help us build long-term industry knowledge?

If a feature satisfies fewer than two principles, do not add it.

## Decision Rules

- Product decisions are more important than data volume.
- Unknown is better than guessed data.
- Manual maintainability is more important than automation at this stage.
- B2B usefulness should be considered before adding new fields.
