## Mission

Build a reliable intelligence system for global LGBTQ+ underwear research, product benchmarking, and B2B customer development.

## Vision

The system should become a practical operating base for identifying product opportunities, understanding competitor positioning, and planning sample development.

## Business Goal

Support the creation of a B2B underwear brand website and product offer that can serve customers interested in custom, wholesale, OEM, and private-label underwear development.

## Long-term Strategy

- Maintain verified brand and product intelligence.
- Convert research into development priorities.
- Build reusable product and market knowledge.
- Use the dashboard to support sales conversations and product roadmap decisions.
